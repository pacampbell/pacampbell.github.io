Steps

  • Speak with Wilson in the Training Chapel
  • Cling to the enemy and successfully use Ride Holy Aura
  • Cling to the enemy and successfully use Ride Heal Aura
  • Use Resist to endure the enemy's swing (yellow icon) while climbing them
  • While climbing the enemy, jump off during their knock-away attack (red icon)
  • Attack and enrage the enemy
  • Reveal the enemy's secret core during their enraged state with Heal Aura
  • Shake the enemy while they are enraged and tire them out!
  • Defeat the Cyclops
  • Collect the proof of completing the trial
  • Report back to Renton in the White Dragon Temple
