Steps

  • Go to Drawan Village and find out how to enter Gardnox Fortress
  • Hear what Sunny has to say in the Sleeping Wolf Inn
  • Head to the Orcs' location
  • Defeat the Orcs to obtain the key to the fortress
  • Pick up the key to Gardnox Fortress
  • Break into Gardnox Fortress by using the key
  • Find a way to enter the heart of the fortress
  • Use the levers to open the main gate of the fortress
  • Head to the back of the main gate and search for the Vessel of Life
  • Defeat Mogok
  • Report back to Leo in the Audience Chamber
