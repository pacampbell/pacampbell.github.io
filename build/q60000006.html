Steps

  • Speak with the White Knight at the base of the cliff before the Training Chapel
  • Speak with Wilson in the Training Chapel
  • Attack an enemy with Shield Blow
  • Change your current Elemental Attribute
  • Use the "Attract" skill to get the enemy's attention
  • Auto-block with your Greatshield
  • Block an enemy attack to charge your Force Gauge
  • Fill your Force Gauge, then attack an enemy with Force Burst
  • Fill in your Force Gauge and Enchant
  • Defeat the remaining Goblins in the inner sanctum
  • Collect the proof of completing the trial
  • Report back to Renton in the White Dragon Temple
