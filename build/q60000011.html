Steps

  • Speak with Wilson in the Training Chapel
  • Attach alchemical stakes to an enemy with Alchemy, and draw their attention.
  • Attach alchemical stakes to an enemy using Cluster, and draw their attention.
  • Amplify the alchemical stakes by attacking them, and draw more enemy attention.
  • Avoid an enemy attack with Alchemical Fuga
  • Attach two masses of alchemical stakes to the enemy.
  • Unleash the power of the alchemical stakes on the enemy with Elixir.
  • Cling to the enemy in the deeper chamber and attack them with Concluster.
  • Defeat the Cyclops
  • Collect the proof of completing the trial
  • Report back to Renton in the White Dragon Temple
