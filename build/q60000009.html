Steps

  • Speak with Wilson in the Training Chapel
  • Capture 2 enemies within Magick Eye: Seeker
  • Attack an enemy with Seeker Arrow
  • Get to the optimum distance away from enemies while using Magick Eye: Seeker, and attack them for more than 3 seconds.
  • Fully charge a Seeker arrow, and loose it at an enemy.
  • Lock onto an ally with Magick Eye: True Aid.
  • Recover an ally's health with True Aid arrows.
  • Recover your health with Healing Shroud.
  • Attack an enemy with Front Kick.
  • Cling to the enemy in the deeper chamber and use Invigorating Magick Arrow.
  • While the enemy is enraged, reveal its secret core with Magick Eye: True Aid
  • Defeat the Cyclops
  • Collect the proof of completing the trial
  • Report back to Renton in the White Dragon Temple
